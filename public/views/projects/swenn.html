About the Project

Client's Requirements

Get the latest news from a variety of your favorite online sources all in one desktop application , easy to read,bookmark and share with people. That has given rise to a News aggregator desktop app with all the features such as personalized feed, sharing articles, bookmarks, adding feeds, etc,.

Details about the project

SwenN is a desktop application to get one's favorite news from a variety of online websites at one spot. Users get to customize the application to get the news/updates from their favorite websites. They can bookmark it for future reviewing, they can also add the news they like in their 'Favorite's' list for future. Apart from all this, users can share the articles they love on social media.
